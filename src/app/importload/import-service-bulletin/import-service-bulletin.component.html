Import Product Service Bulletin Products / Load Service Bulletins


  1. Download the Load Serial Bulletins Template to your local machine.
  2. Enter the product item in column A, row 2 under the Item Name header.
  3. For each product item, enter the beginning date range for the recall in column B under the Begin Date header.
  4. For each product item, enter the ending date range for the recall in column C under the End Date header.
  5. For each product item, enter the beginning serial number for the recall in column D under the Begin SN header.
  6. For each product item, enter the ending serial number for the recall in column E under the End SNheader.
  7. For each product item, enter the product recall message in column F under the Service Bulletin Message header.
  8. For more information, visit the Documentation page.
  9. Save the file in CSV (Comma delimited) format.
  10. Click the Select file button below and locate your CSV file.
  11. Click the Load New Items button to import your information.
